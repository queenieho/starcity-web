Financial District

Ideally located in the middle of the action, Starcity FiDi is a former-commuter's haven with access to the top jobs in the world at your footstep. Couple that with the bustling downtown restaurant scene and you’re where all the action is. This location will be ready for occupancy in Summer 2018.

Union Square

Centrally placed in the heart of San Francisco, this fifty member location is a historic, converted Turkish bath house and will feature amazing communal space, roof deck and walking access to all the best that the city has to offer. This location will be ready for occupancy in Summer 2018.

Jackson Square

The most idyllic neighborhood in San Francisco with Gold Rush-era architecture and quaint tree-lined streets. It’s the oldest established historic district and the future Starcity location honors that tradition. This location will be ready for occupancy in Winter 2019.

Learn More

North Beach

Nestled on the border of Jackson Square and North Beach, this smaller brick and timber conversion will be a sanctuary for the downtown habitant who wants to enjoy an easy stroll to work. This location will be ready for occupancy in Winter 2018.
