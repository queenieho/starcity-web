The Neighborhood

Live in a neighborhood that is constantly evolving and growing. SoMa has grown from an industrial manufacturing hub to the headquarters of some of the most innovative companies in the world.

The character of the factories from the past has created a lasting aesthetic in the neighborhood lending to its raw and earthy streetscapes. Many of the production facilities still remain intact as the "maker culture" has made its resurgence.
